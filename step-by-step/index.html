Colours: your colours. Step 1 of 7

Navigation Bar

⏱ 10 min

Stays visible as visitors scroll. Tells people whose site they're on and lets them jump to any section. Keep it simple — 4 links maximum.

Business name or logo
Menu links (4 max)
Contact or booking button
Stays fixed while scrolling
Hostinger
"Update the nav bar business name to 'your name' and the CTA button to 'your CTA'."
Lovable
"Update the nav logo text to 'your name' and the CTA button to 'your CTA'."
💡 Hostinger tip: if it built from a template, use the chat to update the name and button — those two changes make it feel like yours immediately.
2
Step 2 of 7

Hero Section

⏱ 15 min

The very first thing visitors see. You have 3 seconds. One bold headline, one tagline, one button — don't overcrowd it.

Bold headline
One-line tagline
Single call-to-action button
Background image or colour
Hostinger
"Update the hero. Headline: 'your headline'. Tagline: 'your tagline'. Button: 'your CTA'."
Lovable
"Update the hero headline to 'your headline', tagline to 'your tagline', and button to 'your CTA'."
💡 Ask Claude: "Write 3 hero headlines for my type of business called name. Tone: your tone." Pick your favourite.
✓ Nav + hero = your site is already taking shape.
3
Step 3 of 7

About Section

⏱ 15 min

People buy from people they like and trust. A short honest story builds that trust faster than any list of features.

2–3 sentence story
Why you started
What makes you different
Photo (optional)
Hostinger
"Update the About section text to: [paste your about text from Claude here]. Keep the same layout."
Lovable
"Update the About section body text to: [paste your about text from Claude here]. Keep everything else the same."
💡 Ask Claude: "Write a 3-sentence about section for my type of business called name. Warm and personal tone. Based in Saint John, NB."
4
Step 4 of 7

Services or Menu

⏱ 20 min

Show visitors exactly what you offer. Three options is the sweet spot — too many choices and people stop choosing.

3 service or product cards
Name + short description
Price (optional)
Side-by-side layout
Hostinger
"Update the Services cards. Card 1: service 1. Card 2: service 2. Card 3: service 3."
Lovable
"Update the 3 service cards. Card 1: service 1. Card 2: service 2. Card 3: service 3. Mark card 2 as Most Popular."
💡 Ask Claude: "Write 2-sentence descriptions for these 3 services: service 1, service 2, service 3. Friendly, under 25 words each."
✓ Halfway there — nav, hero, about, services. A complete basic website for most businesses.
5
Step 5 of 7

Testimonials

⏱ 10 min

Real words from real customers are more convincing than anything you write yourself. Even 2–3 quotes make a significant difference.

2–3 customer quotes
Customer name + location
Star rating (optional)
Hostinger
"Update the testimonials. Quote 1: '[your quote]' — [Name, Saint John]. Quote 2: '[your quote]' — [Name, City]. Quote 3: '[your quote]' — [Name, City]."
Lovable
"Update the 3 testimonial quotes. Quote 1: '[your quote]' — [Name, Saint John]. Quote 2: '[your quote]' — [Name, City]. Quote 3: '[your quote]' — [Name, City]. Keep 5 stars above each."
💡 No reviews yet? Use placeholder quotes now and swap them for real ones later. Ask 2–3 happy customers before the workshop day!
6
Step 6 of 7

Call-to-Action Banner

⏱ 10 min

After visitors read your story and reviews, this is where you invite them to act. One clear ask. One button. Nothing else.

One strong headline
Single action button
Contrasting background colour
Hostinger
"Update the CTA banner headline to '[e.g. Ready to get started?]' and the button to 'your CTA'."
Lovable
"Update the CTA banner heading to '[your headline]' and the button to 'your CTA'. Keep the background colour."
💡 Pick ONE action only — book, call, order, or get a quote. Two options here cuts your results in half.
7
Step 7 of 7

Footer

⏱ 10 min

Where people go when they've decided to contact you. Keep it simple — just the essentials. Every business website needs one.

Business name
Phone + email
Social media links
Copyright line
Hostinger
"Update the footer. Business name: your name. Email: your email. Phone: your phone. Copyright: © 2025 your name. Add Instagram and Facebook links."
Lovable
"Update the footer. Name: your name. Email: your email. Phone: your phone. Copyright: © 2025 your name. All rights reserved."
