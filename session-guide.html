Use Claude to Plan Your Website

Claude helps you write your content before you build anything

2
Step 2
Sign Into Claude
Claude
  • 1
    Go to claude.ai in your browser
  • 2
    Click Sign In and choose Continue with Google — this is the fastest way to get started
  • 3
    Once in, you will see a chat box. This is where you type your questions and requests
⚠️ Watch the URL — make sure it says claude.ai. If you search Google for Claude, make sure you click the right result. Look for the URL before clicking.
3
Step 3
Ask Claude to Help Write Your Hero Headline
Claude

Your hero headline is the first thing visitors read — your big hook. If you are not sure what to say, ask Claude to write a few options for you.

Sample prompt to type into Claude
"Write 3 short hero headlines for my business. I run a [describe your business] called [your business name] in Saint John, NB. My customers are [describe them]. The tone should feel [warm / professional / fun / exciting]. Keep each headline under 8 words."
💡 Pick the one that feels most like you. You can always tweak the wording — Claude gives you a starting point, not the final answer.
4
Step 4
Ask Claude to Help Choose Your Colours
Claude

The colours you choose set the mood of your website. If you are not sure where to start, ask Claude for guidance based on how you want customers to feel.

Sample prompt to type into Claude
"What are the best colour schemes for branding a [type of business] that should feel [calm / energetic / trustworthy / creative / luxurious]? Give me 2 or 3 options with colour names."
💡 As Cheryl explained in the session — circles are the most powerful shape in design, and the psychology of colour really matters. Soft blues and greens feel calm and healing. Gold and cream feel warm and premium. Pick what feels like your brand.

4

Send the Master Prompt and Build Your Site

One prompt builds the full page structure — then short adjustments for each section

6
Step 6
Choose Your Builder — Hostinger or Lovable
Your choice

You can use either tool — or try both. As discussed in the session, they are similar but different. Here is the quick version:

Hostinger
Drag and drop editing after the AI builds. Domain and hosting included. Easiest to update yourself. Great for stores. You are locked in to the platform.
Lovable
You own the code. Can connect bookings, payments, databases. Export to GitHub. More flexible long term. Free plan available with 5 credits per day.
💡 Not sure? Start with Hostinger for the easiest experience, or Lovable if you want more flexibility. You can always try both — they are both free to start.
7
Step 7 — Hostinger
Create Your Hostinger Account and Start Building
Hostinger only
  • 1
    Go to hostinger.com and click Get Started
  • 2
    Sign up with your Google account or email address — no credit card needed for the free trial
  • 3
    When prompted, select Create Website
  • 4
    You will see an AI chat box. This is where you will paste your Master Prompt in the next step
  • 5
    If it asks you to choose a plan, ignore the upgrade prompts for now — you can continue on the free trial
⚠️ Hostinger may show you pricing and ask you to buy. Do not pay anything yet. Look for the option to continue with the free trial or use the builder for free.
8
Step 7 — Lovable
Create Your Lovable Account and Start Building
Lovable only
  • 1
    Go to lovable.dev — make sure there is no E at the end: L-O-V-A-B-L-E dot D-E-V
  • 2
    Click Get Started and sign in with your Google account
  • 3
    Click New Project to start a fresh website
  • 4
    You will see a chat box on the left. This is where you paste your Master Prompt
⚠️ If you search Google for Lovable, be careful — there are other sites with similar names. Check the URL says lovable.dev before signing in.
💡 Lovable gives you 5 free credits per day. Each big prompt uses about 1–2 credits. Plan your prompts carefully — one good detailed prompt beats five small ones.
9
Step 8 — The Key Step
Send the Master Prompt
Hostinger or Lovable

This is the most important prompt you will send. It tells the AI everything about your business in one go so it can build the full site structure. You only do this once.

  • 1
    Go back to your prompt guide at the workshop site
  • 2
    Scroll down to find The Master Prompt section — you will see your business details already filled in automatically
  • 3
    Click the Copy button on the Hostinger or Lovable version — whichever tool you are using
  • 4
    Go to your builder and paste it into the chat box — Ctrl + V on Windows or Command + V on Mac
  • 5
    Press Enter and watch the AI build your site
✓ After the master prompt, the AI knows your business. Every prompt after this is just a short adjustment — you do not need to repeat your details.
5

Update Each Section

Use short adjustment prompts to fine-tune each part of your site

10
Step 9
Work Through Sections 1 to 7
Hostinger or Lovable

The prompt guide has a short adjustment prompt for each of the 7 sections. Use these one at a time to update your content. Each prompt is ready to copy and paste.

  • 1
    Nav bar — update your business name and menu button text
  • 2
    Hero section — update your headline, tagline, and button
  • 3
    About section — paste in the text you wrote with Claude. Your origin story, why you started, what makes you different
  • 4
    Services section — update your 3 service or product cards with real names, descriptions, and prices
  • 5
    Testimonials — add real customer quotes, or use placeholders for now and update them later
  • 6
    CTA banner — your one clear call to action. Book, call, get a quote — pick one
  • 7
    Footer — your business name, email, phone, and social media links
💡 As Cheryl said in the session — done is better than perfect. Get the structure up first. You can refine the words and photos any time after.
11
Step 10
Add Your Photos
Hostinger or Lovable

The AI will have put placeholder images in. Replace them with your own photos to make the site feel like yours.

In Hostinger
Click directly on any image in the visual editor. A replace option will appear. Upload your photo from your computer.
In Lovable
Look for the + plus sign at the bottom left of the chat box. Click it to attach your photo. Then type: "Use this as my hero image" or "Replace the about section photo with this."
💡 Save your photos as JPG not PNG for the best results. JPG files are much smaller and load faster. PNG files are better for logos with transparent backgrounds.

Publish Your Site

Get your website live so you can share it with customers

Publishing Your Website

Hostinger

1
Click Publish in the top right of the editor
2
Use the free subdomain for now — yoursite.hostingersite.com
3
Your site is live — copy the link and share it
4
To add a custom domain like yourbusiness.ca, go to Domains in your dashboard

Lovable

1
Click Share in the top right — your site is already live on a lovable.app URL
2
Copy the link and open it in a new tab to see it live
3
To access your code, click the code icon at the top of the editor
4
To add a custom domain, go to Settings → Custom Domain (paid plan required)
