Our Story

Born at the
City Market

It started with a cast iron pan, a family recipe from Brittany, and a Saturday morning spot at the Saint John City Market. What was meant to be a weekend hobby turned into something the lineup wouldn't let us stop.

We keep it simple — fresh batter, local toppings, made in front of you. No rush. Just really good crêpes.
