Platform Comparison

Hostinger vs Lovable — which is right for your business?

hostinger.com

Hostinger

hostinger.com
What's great
  • Visual drag-and-drop editor — no prompting needed after setup
  • E-commerce store built in on the Business plan
  • Domain and hosting included in one monthly price
  • Cheapest long-term option (~$4/month intro)
  • 14-day free trial — no credit card needed
  • AI chat builds the initial site from your description
  • Easy for non-technical people to update independently
Watch out for
  • Proprietary system — you can't export your site code
  • Vendor lock-in — if you leave, the site goes down
  • Intro price requires 48-month upfront payment
  • Renewal price jumps significantly after first term
  • Limited customisation compared to coding from scratch

lovable.dev
What's great
  • Generates real HTML/React code — you own it completely

  • Can add Stripe payments with more flexibility

  • Adding a store requires Stripe + Supabase setup
  • Higher monthly cost than Hostinger if paying

Buying Your Domain

Two recommended options for purchasing yourbusiness.ca separately

⚠️
Should you buy your domain inside Hostinger or Lovable? You can — and it keeps everything in one place. But buying from a separate registrar means you own the domain independently. If you ever switch platforms, your domain comes with you. Either way works — this is just good to know upfront.
godaddy.com

GoDaddy

godaddy.com
~$15–25
per year for .ca or .com domains
  • Most recognisable domain registrar in the world
  • Easy to search and buy — straightforward checkout
  • Good for .ca domains — strong Canadian support
  • 24/7 customer support via phone and chat
  • DNS management is clear and beginner-friendly
  • Can also buy hosting here (not needed if using Hostinger)
⚠️ Watch out: GoDaddy's checkout is notorious for upselling extras — privacy protection, email, security add-ons. Decline everything and just buy the domain. You don't need the extras.
namecheap.com

Namecheap

namecheap.com
~$10–18
per year — often cheaper than GoDaddy
  • Often cheaper than GoDaddy, especially on renewal
  • Free WhoisGuard privacy protection included
  • Clean, no-nonsense checkout — less upselling
  • Well-regarded in the developer community
  • Slightly more technical interface than GoDaddy
  • Good DNS management panel
💡 Recommended for: Anyone who wants to save a few dollars and get free privacy protection included without being upsold at checkout.

Connecting Your Domain

Step-by-step instructions for Hostinger and Lovable

Option A — Buy domain inside Hostinger (easiest)
  • 1
    Go to your Hostinger dashboard
    Log in at hostinger.com → click Domains in the left sidebar
  • 2
    Search for your domain
    Type yourbusiness.ca in the search box and click Search. Try .ca first for a Canadian business — it builds local trust.
  • 3
    Purchase and connect
    Click Add to Cart → complete checkout. Hostinger will automatically connect the domain to your site — no DNS setup needed.
    ✓ Done — your domain is live within minutes
Option B — Bring your own domain from GoDaddy or Namecheap
  • 1
    Get your Hostinger nameservers
    In Hostinger dashboard → Hosting → Manage → DNS / Nameservers. Copy the two nameserver addresses shown (they look like ns1.dns-parking.com)
  • 2
    Log into your domain registrar
    Go to GoDaddy or Namecheap → find your domain → click Manage DNS or Nameservers
  • 3
    Replace the nameservers
    Click Change Nameservers → delete the existing ones → paste in the two Hostinger nameservers you copied in Step 1
  • 4
    Add domain inside Hostinger
    Back in Hostinger → Domains → Add Domain → type your domain name and click Connect
  • 5
    Wait for DNS to propagate
    DNS changes take 1–24 hours to fully activate. Most are live within a few hours.
    ⏱ Be patient — this part is out of your hands
Option A — Buy domain inside Lovable (easiest)
  • 1
    Open your project settings
    In Lovable editor → click the three dots menu top right → Settings → Domains
  • 2
    Click "Buy a Domain"
    Search for your preferred domain name. Lovable uses Entri to handle domain registration — it's built into the flow.
  • 3
    Complete purchase and publish
    Once purchased, Lovable connects the domain automatically. Click Publish to make your site live.
    ⚠️ Requires a paid Lovable plan ($25/month Pro)
Option B — Connect domain from GoDaddy or Namecheap
  • 1
    Go to Lovable Settings → Domains
    In your project → Settings → Domains → Connect Domain → type your domain name → click Continue
  • 2
    Select your DNS provider
    Lovable will ask which provider you use — select GoDaddy or Namecheap. It will then show you the exact DNS records you need to add.
  • 3
    Add the CNAME record at your registrar
    Log into GoDaddy or Namecheap → Manage DNS → Add a new CNAME record pointing to the address Lovable gave you
    CNAME → www → your-project.lovable.app
  • 4
    Verify in Lovable
    Back in Lovable Settings → click Verify. Lovable will automatically issue an SSL certificate (the padlock in the browser).
  • 5
    Wait and publish
    DNS changes can take up to 24 hours. Once the domain shows as Live in settings, click Publish to go live.
    ✓ Your site is live at yourbusiness.ca
